We forget that we have a full fledged browser always running which is a great software to do anything. In fact I have composed Gmail drafts and sometimes typed in Google to temporarily note down phone numbers and addresses. I found a trick to turn the browser quickly into a text editor/notepad

  1. Open a new browser tab

  2. Type the following in the address bar and hit Enter

    data:text/html,<html contenteditable>
