I spoke about rich client technologies like JavaFx and HTML5 while Dr. Amit Kumar briefed about IEEE activities and membership benefits to the students . It was long 5 hours of drive from Hyderabad but worth visiting because the college is situated at a very distant area from the IT industry of Hyderabad and even a slightest exposure can inspire the students.

This college was not only have same name as my under grad college but also situated at a similar rural location. I like this kind of residential college because you have not much distraction of city life and bound to focus on studies :)
