Apart of excellent teaching facility I saw so many Vegetable and fruit plants in the campus that it could feed all the children and staffs. There were huge solar panels providing power to the street lights and guest houses.  Construction material were manufactured in the campus itself. There was a cowshed with cows and  bio gas plant as well.
