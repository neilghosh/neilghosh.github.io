Online collaborative spreadsheet like Google Docs  (with appropriate template )can be shared easily within friends but they can end up with messy calculation when a lot of people are involved and not having equal share in paying and owing.

BillMonk tracks shared expenses within social groups - friends, co-workers and family.




Similar to iOWEYOU, BillMonk helps you cover or borrow from your friends without worrying that one of you will forget.

  • Borrow and remember. Don't forget to pay your friend back for the cash she loaned you the other day.

  • Roommates. Rent, phone, power... solve the headache of figuring out everyone's fair share.

  • Restaurants. It's stressful to split restaurant bills. Just let one person pay and report it

  • Group vacations. Tracking who paid for what on vacation is no fun: hotel, gas, tickets, etc. Report these bills on the go with your cell phone and settle up when you get home!

  • Shared Gifts. Make it easy to track debts when a group of friends pitch in together to buy a nice gift for someone.
