--- layout: post title: Remove or Change Proxy settings for apt in Ubuntu date: '2011-04-25T17:47:00.000+05:30' author: Neil Ghosh tags: - Linux - TIP - Ubuntu - http - apt - proxy modified_time: '2011-07-07T05:02:22.059+05:30' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6081677503074893817.post-3361505836232270696 blogger_orig_url: https://www.neilghosh.com/2011/04/remove-or-change-proxy-settings-for-apt.html --- Remove proxy settings from apt?
Try to edit your /etc/apt/apt.conf
and change it to:
Code:

Acquire::http::Proxy "false";
