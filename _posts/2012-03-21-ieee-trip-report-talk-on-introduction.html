There were two sessions. 1st session was by me. It was about "Introduction to HTML5". I took a part of the HTML5 demo slides from html5rocks.com. Interesting thing about these slides is that they were purely based on HTML/CSS/javaScript and runs on a browser. So there was no traditional power points presentation . This also enabled the inline demos and I did not have to carry extra HTML files for demo. The Demo worked best on the latest version of Google Chrome but most of them was compatible with Mozilla Firefox also. The detailed compatibility chart can found here . In fact you can test your own browser by visiting this website.





The second session was on the lines of core Computer Science. Tiru took a session on "Contributions of Allan Tuning". Allan Tuning is a legendary mathematician who invented Tuning Machine.
