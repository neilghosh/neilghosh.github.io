# Web Tools & Applications

A collection of web-based tools and applications I've built to solve everyday problems:

## Audio & Video Tools

- **[Record Audio](record-audio/index.html)**  
  Browser-based audio recording tool perfect for microphone testing, voice notes, and quick recordings. No installation required.

- **[Video Tiles](https://neilghosh.com/video-tiles/)**  
  Watch multiple YouTube videos simultaneously in a single browser window. Great for comparing content or monitoring multiple streams.

## AI-Powered Applications

- **[Identify Birds](https://birding.web.app)**  
  Upload a bird photograph and get instant identification with names in multiple Indian languages. Perfect for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts.

- **[Stargazing App](https://stargazing.web.app)**  
  Comprehensive astronomical companion featuring upcoming celestial events, sunrise/sunset times, moon phases, and Indian calendar integration.

- **[News Podcast](https://newscast.web.app)**  
  AI-generated personalized news podcast that curates and narrates news stories based on your interests. Get your daily briefing in audio format.
